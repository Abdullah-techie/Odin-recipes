Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • Red bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • ginger
  • sunflower oil
  • Rice
  • Dried thyme
  • Paprika powder
  • Nutmeg
  • Bay leaf
  • salt
  • vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes,onions,bell pepper,ginger garlic and puree in a blender
  2. Cook the pureed mixture in a pot with hot cooking oil over medium-high heat. stir together and place a lid over it,reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Stir in the spices and seasonings(Paprika,thyme,nutmeg,bay leaf and salt) then add rice and vegetable or meat stock and stir together.
  4. place a piece of alluminium foil into the pot,covering the entire surface of the rice, then place a lid over the pot.Cook for 20 minutes on low heat.
  5. Remove the lid,in the alluminium foil and give the rice a gentle stir, then return the lid and cook for another 5 minutes.
