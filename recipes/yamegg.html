Yam with Fried egg sauce

Ingredients

  • 4-6 eggs
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 chopped scotch bonnet
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/3 teaspoon salt
  • 2 big yams
  • 1/2 tsp of curry and thyme
  • vegetable oil

Steps(1)

  1. slice the yams into about 1-inch size and peel the skin off
  2. put the peeled yam in a pan,add enough water to cover it,add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft

    3. Steps(2)

  3. Break egg into a shallow bowl,and whisk till egg white and yolk are well combined. Then set it aside
  4. place a frying pan on a low heat,add of vegetable oil,heat fr about 3 minutes
  5. Add chopped pepper and onions and cook for about 4-5 minutes or till oil flows on the sauce,add salt,seasoning curry and thyme.stir to combine ingredients with sauce
  6. Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute,this will allow
