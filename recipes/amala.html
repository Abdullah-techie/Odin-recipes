Amala

Ingredients

  • yam flour(elubo)
  • locust beans
  • grinded pepper
  • palm oil
  • salt
  • egusi vegetable
  • water
  • maggi

Steps

  1. pour water into a mettalic pot and put it on a cooker for it to boil
  2. seive the yam flour powder to ensure there's no impurity in it
  3. After the water has boiled,pour the powder in it and stir it with a small pestle
  4. continue stirring until all the powder have been moistured
  5. now,your amala is ready.but there are other steps to take to make the dish complete
  6. get your frying pan ready and put it on the cooker. Then add the following serially: palm oil,locust bean and your grinded pepper
  7. then stir it and add appreciable quantity of salt and maggi
  8. keep stirring gently for 2 minutes,then add your egusi to it
  9. for an unlimited enjoyment,add a stock of meat and serve yourself
